Welcome!

Can you get a high score on this Marijuana quiz?

This is a fun and simple quiz game to test your knowledge of the magical plant of Marijuanna.
You will find 10 questions with multiple-choices answers to choose from. These questions cover a wide range of topics related to Marijuana, including questions about history, potency, political status and more.
Be sure to have fun playing this interactive game, learn more about the herb and be cannabis savvy! This amazing quiz can be used for educational and activist purposes.

Instructions

Click on the "Play" button below to start the quiz.
Read the questions carefully. Simply select your chosen answer for each of the questions.
Correct answer will turn Green, Incorrect answers will turn Red.
Dont forget to save your highscore when you have completed the quiz!
